# Sync Google Drive to Wasabi — Affordable Archive and Backup with RcloneView

> Google Drive is convenient for active collaboration, but long-term storage costs add up. Wasabi offers S3-compatible hot storage at half the price—perfect for archiving your Google Drive with RcloneView.

Google Drive is ideal for team collaboration, but unlimited storage comes with retention complexity. Wasabi provides hot cloud storage with predictable pricing and no egress fees. RcloneView automates the process of archiving completed projects, older files, and cold data to Wasabi.

## Connect Google Drive and Wasabi

Setting up both remotes in RcloneView is quick and secure.

**Google Drive:**
1. Click **Add Remote** → Select **Google Drive**
2. Authorize RcloneView via OAuth
3. Grant folder/file access permissions
4. Verify connection

**Wasabi:**
1. Click **Add Remote** → Select **Wasabi**
2. Enter your Wasabi Access Key and Secret Key
3. Select your bucket and region
4. Test connectivity

![New Remote Setup](/images/en/blog/new-remote.png)

## Archive Google Drive to Wasabi Hot Storage

Create a one-time or recurring sync job to move your files. Wasabi's hot storage is immediately accessible—no restore delays like glacier.

**Archive scenarios:**
- **Project completion**: Archive client deliverables after project ends
- **Storage optimization**: Move files older than 90 days to Wasabi
- **Compliance backup**: Keep searchable copies of business records

## Getting Started

1. **Download RcloneView** from [rcloneview.com](https://rcloneview.com/src/download.html).
2. Create a Wasabi account at [wasabi.com](https://wasabi.com/).
3. Add Google Drive as a remote using OAuth authentication.
4. Configure your Wasabi bucket as a remote.
5. Create a sync or copy job and start archiving.
6. Schedule regular backups to keep archives current.

Archive affordably, retrieve instantly—Wasabi and RcloneView make it simple.
