# Migrate OneDrive for Business to Google Shared Drives — Enterprise File Transfer with RcloneView

> Entire departments of files on OneDrive for Business need to land on Google Shared Drives. Manual download-upload is impossible at scale. RcloneView handles it visually, in batches, with verification.

Enterprise migrations between OneDrive for Business and Google Shared Drives are increasingly common as organizations consolidate platforms. The scale is the challenge — hundreds of users, terabytes of data, and the need for zero data loss. RcloneView connects both platforms natively and provides the tools for controlled, verified migration.

## Post-Migration

- Keep OneDrive active for 2-4 weeks as a transition buffer
- Run final Folder Comparison
- Redirect users to Google Drive
- Decommission OneDrive accounts
