# Manage Citrix ShareFile — Sync and Backup Enterprise Files with RcloneView

> ShareFile powers enterprise collaboration—RcloneView gives you complete control over your business documents.

Citrix ShareFile is the enterprise standard for secure file sharing, collaboration, and document management. However, managing backups, syncing to other cloud platforms, and maintaining data governance can be complex. RcloneView streamlines ShareFile management, enabling automated backups and multi-cloud synchronization in minutes.

## Why Sync ShareFile Beyond Citrix?

While ShareFile excels at collaboration, many organizations need their documents in multiple places: backup to cold storage, disaster recovery systems, compliance archives, or analysis platforms. RcloneView enables these workflows without manual intervention or complex APIs.

<img src="/support/images/en/blog/new-remote.png" alt="Connecting Citrix ShareFile as a remote in RcloneView" class="img-large img-center" />

## Setting Up ShareFile in RcloneView

Connecting ShareFile to RcloneView requires just your ShareFile credentials:

1. Open RcloneView and select "Add Remote"
2. Choose Citrix ShareFile from the provider list
3. Authenticate with your ShareFile account
4. Authorize RcloneView access to your file library
5. Name your remote and confirm

Your ShareFile instance is now accessible through RcloneView's file explorer.

<img src="/support/images/en/blog/cloud-to-cloud-transfer-default.png" alt="Syncing ShareFile documents to multiple cloud destinations" class="img-large img-center" />

## Automated Enterprise Backups

Set up scheduled backups to store ShareFile documents in AWS S3, Google Cloud Storage, or Azure Blob. Create daily incremental backups that only transfer changed files, minimizing bandwidth and storage costs. Track all backup jobs in RcloneView's history dashboard.

<img src="/support/images/en/howto/rcloneview-basic/job-history.png" alt="Viewing ShareFile backup job history and status" class="img-large img-center" />

## Compliance and Disaster Recovery

Maintain immutable copies of critical documents for audit trails and regulatory compliance. RcloneView's versioning support ensures you can recover any file version from any point in time, protecting against accidental deletions or ransomware attacks.

## Getting Started

1. **Ensure your ShareFile administrator** has enabled API access for your account.
2. **Download RcloneView** from [rcloneview.com](https://rcloneview.com/src/download.html).
3. **Add ShareFile** and authenticate with your corporate credentials.
4. **Schedule backups** to your preferred cloud destination.

Protect your enterprise data with the confidence that RcloneView provides.
