# Manage IDrive e2 — S3-Compatible Cloud Backup with RcloneView

> IDrive e2 offers S3-compatible storage at a fraction of the cost—RcloneView makes managing it effortless.

IDrive e2 is a cost-effective, S3-compatible object storage platform that provides enterprise-grade reliability without the premium price tag. If you're looking to reduce cloud storage costs while maintaining full backup and sync capabilities, RcloneView integrates seamlessly with IDrive e2 to automate your entire backup workflow.

## Why IDrive e2 for Cloud Backup?

IDrive e2 delivers S3 API compatibility, meaning it works with any tool that speaks S3—including RcloneView. With competitive pricing and multiple data center options, it's ideal for companies managing large-scale backups without overspending. Whether you're backing up databases, media libraries, or entire file systems, IDrive e2 scales to meet your needs.

<img src="/support/images/en/blog/new-remote.png" alt="Adding IDrive e2 as a new remote in RcloneView" class="img-large img-center" />

## Setting Up IDrive e2 in RcloneView

RcloneView treats IDrive e2 like any other S3-compatible storage. Simply:

1. Open RcloneView and add a new remote
2. Select S3-compatible as the provider
3. Enter your IDrive e2 endpoint URL and credentials
4. Name your remote and test the connection

Within minutes, you'll have full access to your IDrive e2 buckets through RcloneView's intuitive interface.

<img src="/support/images/en/blog/cloud-to-cloud-transfer-default.png" alt="Transferring data to IDrive e2 buckets" class="img-large img-center" />

## Automating Backups to IDrive e2

Use RcloneView's job scheduler to create recurring backups to IDrive e2. Set hourly, daily, or weekly backups from your local storage or other cloud providers. Monitor transfer progress in real-time and receive alerts when jobs complete or fail.

<img src="/support/images/en/howto/rcloneview-advanced/create-job-schedule.png" alt="Scheduling recurring backup jobs to IDrive e2" class="img-large img-center" />

## Disaster Recovery Made Simple

Because IDrive e2 is versioned and geographically distributed, you'll have multiple recovery points. RcloneView lets you quickly restore files, entire folders, or complete datasets whenever you need them.

## Getting Started

1. **Sign up for IDrive e2** and obtain your access key, secret key, and endpoint URL.
2. **Download RcloneView** from [rcloneview.com](https://rcloneview.com/src/download.html).
3. **Add IDrive e2 as a remote** using the S3-compatible provider option.
4. **Schedule your first backup** and let RcloneView handle the rest.

Start protecting your data affordably with IDrive e2 and RcloneView today.
