# Manage pCloud Storage — Sync and Backup Files with RcloneView

> Keep your pCloud files in perfect sync and always backed up with automated cloud storage management.

Managing personal cloud storage across multiple devices can be overwhelming. pCloud offers excellent encryption and European hosting, but synchronizing files, maintaining backups, and organizing data across locations requires the right tools. RcloneView transforms pCloud management with intuitive sync operations, automated backups, and scheduled tasks that keep your files consistent everywhere.

## Why Sync and Backup pCloud?

pCloud's zero-knowledge encryption makes it ideal for privacy-conscious users. However, managing multiple pCloud folders across devices, maintaining local backups, or syncing with other services requires manual work. RcloneView automates these processes, letting you set sophisticated backup strategies without complexity.

<img src="/support/images/en/blog/new-remote.png" alt="Add new pCloud remote in RcloneView" class="img-large img-center" />

## Setting Up pCloud in RcloneView

Connecting pCloud takes minutes. RcloneView provides guided setup that securely authenticates with your pCloud account. Once configured, your pCloud storage appears in the remote explorer, showing folder structure, file sizes, and modification dates for complete visibility.

<img src="/support/images/en/blog/cloud-to-cloud-transfer-default.png" alt="pCloud sync interface in RcloneView" class="img-large img-center" />

## Automated Sync and Scheduled Backups

Create sync jobs that run on your schedule. Sync pCloud to external drives, backup to other clouds, or keep multiple accounts synchronized. RcloneView's scheduler lets you set frequency, time windows, and specific folders—then handles everything automatically.

<img src="/support/images/en/howto/rcloneview-advanced/create-job-schedule.png" alt="Schedule backup jobs in RcloneView" class="img-large img-center" />

## Getting Started

1. **Download RcloneView** from [rcloneview.com](https://rcloneview.com/src/download.html).
2. **Add pCloud remote** by selecting pCloud from remote explorer and authorizing your account.
3. **Create a sync job** between pCloud and your backup destination.
4. **Schedule automatic runs** to keep backups current with zero manual effort.

Protect your pCloud data today with intelligent, automated backup strategies.
